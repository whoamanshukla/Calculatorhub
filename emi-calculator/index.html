About This EMI Calculator

This EMI Calculator helps you estimate your Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) for loans such as home loans, personal loans, car loans and more. Just enter the loan amount, annual interest rate and loan tenure to get instant results.

How to Use the EMI Calculator

  1. Enter your total loan amount.
  2. Enter the annual interest rate.
  3. Enter the loan tenure in years.
  4. Click on the Calculate EMI button.
  5. View your monthly EMI, total interest and total repayment amount.

EMI Formula

EMI is calculated using the standard formula:

EMI = [P × R × (1 + R)N] / [(1 + R)N - 1]

Where:

  • P = Loan amount
  • R = Monthly interest rate
  • N = Number of monthly installments

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EMI?

EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It is the fixed monthly amount you pay to repay a loan over a specific period.

Can I use this EMI Calculator for home loans?

Yes, you can use it for home loans, car loans, personal loans and other fixed-rate loans.

Does a longer loan tenure reduce EMI?

Yes, a longer tenure usually lowers the monthly EMI, but it may increase the total interest paid over time.
